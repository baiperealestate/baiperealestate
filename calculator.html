Additional Information – Residents vs. Non-Residents (Indicative Figures)

The estimated results of this calculator should be interpreted using the following general financing and purchase cost ranges commonly applied in Curaçao. These figures are indicative only and may vary by lender.

Mortgage Financing – Typical Ranges

Residents of Curaçao
  • Loan-to-Value (LTV): up to 80% – 90% of the property value (Full (100%) financing is not standard)
  • Down Payment: typically 0% – 20%
  • Mortgage Term: up to 35 years, subject to age and income
  • Indicative Interest Rates: approximately 4% – 6% (variable or fixed, depending on lender)

Non-Residents
  • Loan-to-Value (LTV): typically 60% – 70%
  • Down Payment: typically 30% – 40%
  • Mortgage Term: often shorter than resident mortgages
  • Indicative Interest Rates: generally higher than resident rates, depending on lender and risk assessment

Financing approval, terms, and interest rates are determined solely by the bank and depend on income source, credit profile, age, residency status, and internal lending policies.
